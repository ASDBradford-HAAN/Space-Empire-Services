# Space Empire Services

## Mission

Provide practical AI governance, risk assessment, research, and strategic analysis services while supporting the long-term development of The Space Empire ecosystem.

## Initial Services

### AI Governance Readiness Assessment

A structured review of an organization's AI usage, governance practices, documentation readiness, and regulatory risk exposure.

### AI Regulatory Risk Snapshot

A non-legal assessment identifying potential governance and compliance risks associated with AI systems.

### Strategic Risk Analysis

Decision-support reports identifying operational, governance, economic, and technology risks.
